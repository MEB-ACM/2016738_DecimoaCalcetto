# 2016738_DecimoaCalcetto
Laboratory of Advanced Programming's Project

Decimo a Calcetto is a software product designed for amateur football enthusiasts. The platform simplifies the organization and management of casual football matches by helping players, team and referees connect seamlessly. The system enables users to create, join, and manage matches efficiently, ensuring that teams are always complete and ready to play. 

Execution:
- docker-compose up --build
After this you can go on localhost:5500 and can navigate the site
