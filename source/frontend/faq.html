FAQ

How can I join a match?

To join a match, go to the match details page and click on the JOIN button. You will need to confirm your participation.

Can I cancel my participation?

Yes, you can cancel by going to your profile and selecting the match you joined, then clicking "Cancel Participation".

How can I become a referee?

You can apply to become a referee via the "Become Referee" section. You will need to provide your personal details and experience.

How do I organize a new match?

To organize a match, go to the "Organize Game" section, fill in the details such as date, time, location, and skill level, then publish it so that other players can join.

Is there a cost to use the platform?

Joining and organizing matches is free. However, field booking fees are handled directly with the facility and may vary depending on the venue.

Can I rate players after a match?

Yes! After each match, you can leave feedback about other players. This helps keep the community respectful and enjoyable for everyone.
